Fried rice is one of the foods that is quite popular in Indonesia and is a favorite dish for many people. This dish is made from fried rice with various ingredients and spices, resulting in a savory and delicious taste. Fried rice is usually served as a main dish in the morning, afternoon or evening.

The following is a simple recipe for making fried rice that you can try at home:

Ingredients:

• 2 cups of cooked rice

• 2 eggs, shake off

• 3 shallots, finely chopped

• 2 cloves of garlic, finely chopped

• 1 spring onion, finely sliced

• 3 tablespoons of cooking oil

• 2 tablespoons of sweet soy sauce

• 1 tablespoon chili sauce

• Salt to taste

• Pepper powder to taste

How to make:

1. Heat cooking oil in a skillet.

2. Stir-fry the red onion and the garlic until you can smell the scent.

3. Add beaten eggs and stir until cooked.

4. Add rice and mix well.

5. Add sweet soy sauce and chili sauce. Stir well.

6. Add salt and pepper powder to taste.

7. Cook fried rice until cooked and looks a bit dry.

8. Sprinkle spring onions over the fried rice.

9. Lift and serve.

You can also try variations of fried rice, such as seafood fried rice, goat fried rice, or chicken fried rice. You can add ingredients according to your taste, such as shrimp, squid, beef or chicken, as well as vegetables such as carrots and peas.

The benefits of this fried rice are quite a lot, besides containing high carbohydrates, fried rice also contains protein from eggs and other additional ingredients. However, keep in mind that excessive fried rice and consumed too often can cause weight gain and increase the risk of cardiovascular disease.

That's a simple recipe for making fried rice that you can try at home. Good luck and good luck!
