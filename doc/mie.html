Fried noodles are a food that is quite popular in Indonesia and also in several other countries in Asia. This dish is made of noodles that are fried with various ingredients and spices, resulting in a savory and delicious taste. Fried noodles are usually served as a main dish in the morning, afternoon or evening.

The following is a simple recipe for making fried noodles that you can try at home:

Ingredients:

• 1 pack of instant noodles

• 2 eggs, shake off

• 3 shallots, finely chopped

• 2 cloves of garlic, finely chopped

• 1 spring onion, finely sliced

• 2 tablespoons of cooking oil

• 1 tablespoon sweet soy sauce

• 1 tablespoon of tomato sauce

• Salt to taste

• Pepper powder to taste

How to make:

1. Boil instant noodles until cooked. Once cooked, drain the water.

2. Heat cooking oil in a skillet.

3. Stir-fry the red onion and the garlic until you can smell the scent.

4. Add beaten eggs and stir until cooked.

5. Add instant noodles to the wok and mix well.

6. Add sweet soy sauce and tomato sauce. Stir well.

7. Add salt and ground pepper to taste.

8. Cook the fried noodles until cooked and looks a bit dry.

9. Sprinkle spring onions over the fried noodles.

10. Lift and serve.

You can also try variations of fried noodles, such as seafood fried noodles, spicy fried noodles, or special fried noodles. You can add ingredients according to taste, such as shrimp, squid, beef or chicken, as well as vegetables such as carrots and peas.

The benefits of fried noodles are quite a lot, apart from being high in carbohydrates, fried noodles also contain protein from eggs and other additives. But keep in mind that excessive and frequently consumed fried noodles can cause weight gain and increase the risk of cardiovascular disease.
